Explore The Programmes

Full Stack Development Internship

Rootaxis offers internships for graduates, including full stack development. Interns work on live projects, receive mentorship from experienced developers, and gain exposure to cutting-edge technologies. The program is highly competitive but provides valuable real-world experience.

BlockChain Development Internship

Rootaxis provides blockchain development internships where students get hands-on experience with rootaxis Blockchain, Hyperledger Fabric, and related technologies. Interns work on real projects in areas like supply chain, finance, and healthcare, learning about enterprise blockchain.

AI / ML Developer Internship

Rootaxis offers one of the most sought-after AI internships, focusing on deep learning, reinforcement learning, and artificial general intelligence (AGI) research. Interns work with cutting-edge AI models such as GPT and DALL-E, contributing to high-impact projects that push the AI capabilities.

Devops Internship

Rootaxis provides DevOps internships where students work with Azure,aws cloud infrastructure and DevOps tools like Kubernetes, and Docker.
